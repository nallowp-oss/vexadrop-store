# VexaDrop Store Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
VexaDrop represents the intersection of high-performance AI and premium lifestyle. The brand is sophisticated, futuristic, and elite.

## Design Principles
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist yet vibrant. High-end tech vibes.
- **Glassmorphism**: Use translucent layers and deep blurs to create depth.
- **Typography**: Primary font: 'Outfit', Secondary: 'Inter'.
- **Color Palette**:
  - Base: `#0a0a0a` (Deep Obsidian)
  - Accent 1: `#ff3366` (Vibrant Pink)
  - Accent 2: `#7000ff` (Circuit Purple)
  - Text: `#ffffff` (Pure White), `#9ca3af` (Secondary Gray)

## Voice and Tone
- **Tone**: Sophisticated, professional, and forward-thinking.
- **Interaction**: AI agents should be polite but authoritative, acting as elite concierges.
